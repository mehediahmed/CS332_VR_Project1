DIY Cave Environment
Author: Ryan Blanchard
email: r_y_a_n__b@hotmail.com

This package is a collection of models that allow you to build your own cave environments.  It is made up of tunnels, walls, floors, and ceilings to allow you to make environments as big or small as you need.  Also included are some props and particle effects to help you get started at adding life to your caves.

An example of some rooms and tunnels is included.

The package contains:
63 unique models 
126 LOD models (reduced poly versions of the 63 unique models)
4 particle effects
Example rooms and tunnels
